10th century

Lehenga

The lehenga, originating in the Mughal era, symbolizes opulence and elegance with its luxurious fabrics and intricate embroidery. Evolving over centuries, it remains a beloved traditional attire, cherished for its timeless beauty and cultural significance, worn at weddings, festivals, and special occasions across India.

17th century

Dhakai Jamdani

Dhakai Jamdani, originating from Dhaka, Bangladesh, is a fine muslin fabric intricately woven with intricate motifs using the supplementary weft technique. Known for its sheer texture, lightweight feel, and exquisite designs, Dhakai Jamdani is prized for its craftsmanship and cultural significance, representing a timeless legacy of artisanal excellence.
